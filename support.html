Frequently Asked Questions

How does SkyReady determine its recommendations?

SkyReady uses Apple WeatherKit data for your current location, analysing temperature, precipitation probability, wind speed, UV index, and conditions to give you a simple, actionable recommendation rather than overwhelming you with raw data.

Why does SkyReady need my location?

SkyReady uses your location to fetch accurate weather data for where you are right now. Your location is only used to request weather data and is never stored on our servers or shared with third parties.

How do I add saved locations?

Tap the Locations button on the main screen, then tap the + button to search for and add any location. You can save multiple locations and quickly switch between them.

How do I set up weather notifications?

Tap the bell icon in the top-right corner of the main screen to configure daily weather notifications. You can choose what time you'd like to receive your daily weather briefing.

SkyReady Premium

What's included in Premium?

SkyReady Premium includes the extended 14-day forecast, multiple saved locations, and daily weather notifications. Free users get the core weather recommendation experience.

How do I restore my subscription?

If you've previously subscribed and need to restore your purchase, go to Settings (gear icon) and tap Restore Purchases. This will sync your subscription status with the App Store.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone to manage or cancel your SkyReady subscription. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.

Troubleshooting

Weather data isn't loading

  • Check that Location Services are enabled for SkyReady in Settings > Privacy > Location Services
  • Ensure you have an active internet connection
  • Try closing and reopening the app

Notifications aren't arriving

  • Check that notifications are enabled in Settings > Notifications > SkyReady
  • Ensure Background App Refresh is enabled for SkyReady
  • Verify your notification time is set correctly in the app
